 Bill Elliott was born on October 8th, 1955, in Dawsonville, Georgia. The retired NASCAR driver has won over 40 races in the Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series along with 320 top tens and 55 poles. Over the past 37 years, Bill Elliott has been involved in hundreds of races. With his performances came a lot of fame; Not only was he seen as one of the best by his peers, such as Cale Yarborough and Bobby Allison, but by the fans, too. NASCAR themselves even inducted Elliott into the NASCAR Hall of Fame in 2015, and the driver has been awarded NASCAR’s Most Popular Driver Award a record 16 times, more than any other driver. Since the mid-80s, when Elliott became a common face among the top ten, NASCAR fans around the country have made “Bill Elliott” a household name, due to not only his achievements, but his laid back attitude and demeanor.
